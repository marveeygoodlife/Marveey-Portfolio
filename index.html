Marvellous Ediagbonya Website Developer

Hello! I am a Student and a Website Developer. I am from Edo State Nigeria.
I have always been curious to know how the web work, I have often asked myself; "How's it that there are different websites only? How does it get there? Can I do something similar?. I often ask myself this question until I researched and found out how it works. I was intrigued and since then my passion for it has increased, I made a vow to make interactive, User-friendly and stylish website..
To master this skill I decide to learn about HTML, CSS and Javascript to understand how the client side of web development works before I will go on to master the server side.

About Me

As someone passionate about web development, I like the creativity and problem-solving that comes with building stylish and user friendly website. I love that I can turn a challenge into a dynamic experience online. From writing Html code to styling with Css to ensure it's beautiful, I take pride in every step of the development process.
I am someone that loves to learn from others, so collaborating with other developers to brainstorm, debugg, share ideas and build something grater than what one person can do is a goal that I lok forward to. I believe you one can achieve excelent result by exchanging of knowledge, constructive feedback and mutual support. I'm commited to working in a team to achieve outstanding success.

Aside Web development I am interested in learning, Reading books, Travelling, living healthy, staying fit and helping people in anyway I can.
